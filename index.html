FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is access available to anyone who submits a form?+
No. Access to Affiliate Warehouse shared trade accounts is never automatic. All applications are individually reviewed and approved based on suitability, compliance, and alignment with our standards. Only approved preferred contractors may access the accounts and receive work from us. Submitting an application does not guarantee approval in any circumstance.
What wholesaler accounts do I get access to?+
Approved preferred contractors gain access through TradeZone, Ideal Electrical, and Voltex accounts. These are the same supplier systems you may already use ‚Äî the difference is access through Affiliate Warehouse's corporate-level accounts, which may unlock volume-based pricing structures subject to supplier approval and terms.
How does Affiliate Warehouse make money?+
Affiliate Warehouse retains 1.1% of any verified discount achieved on a purchase. If no discount is achieved, there is no charge whatsoever. Fees are billed monthly and apply only where savings have been verifiably confirmed. There are no subscription fees, access costs, or charges based on order volume.
Am I locked in once approved?+
No. Once approved, there are no contracts, no minimum spend, and no exclusivity requirements. Approved preferred contractors can stop using Affiliate Warehouse at any time with no exit fees or penalties. Your original supplier accounts remain entirely unaffected.
How do I know my pricing is actually improving?+
As a preferred contractor you can run a side-by-side comparison against your original trade accounts at any time. The system tracks pricing over time on repeat purchases, so improvements ‚Äî or increases ‚Äî are clearly visible and based on actual data, not claims.
Is this fully compliant with my supplier systems?+
Yes. Affiliate Warehouse operates within existing wholesaler systems and terms. All purchases use the same backend access and traceability as your regular accounts. Purchases remain job-coded and processed through normal wholesaler channels. No supplier terms are bypassed or altered.
What if it stops being useful for my business?+
Simply stop using it. There are no exit clauses, no fees for leaving, and no minimum periods. Your original supplier accounts are entirely unaffected at all times.
How are better prices actually achieved?+
Better pricing may be achieved through aggregated purchasing volume across our independent preferred contractors nationally. By consistently purchasing together within existing wholesaler systems, the network can access pricing tiers typically reserved for larger operators ‚Äî subject to supplier approval and prevailing market conditions.
